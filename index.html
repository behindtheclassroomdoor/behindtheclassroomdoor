About

I’m a primary school educator who has spent years working inside classrooms, staff rooms and playgrounds across modern education.

During that time I’ve seen the extraordinary dedication of teachers, the hopes parents carry for their children, and the quiet pressures shaping schools behind the scenes.

I began writing to open an honest conversation about what is really happening in primary education today — the good, the difficult, and the things we rarely say out loud.
