Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used and protected when you visit this website.

Who We Are

This website is operated by the author of Behind the Classroom Door.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can contact:
contact@behindtheclassroomdoor.co.uk

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use this website:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Messages you send via the contact form or email
  • Basic website usage data such as pages visited and time spent on the site

This information is only collected when you voluntarily provide it or when you browse the website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to enquiries or messages
  • Provide information about books or related content
  • Improve the website and understand how visitors use it
  • Maintain website security

Your personal information will never be sold or shared for marketing purposes.

Website Analytics

This website may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site.

These tools collect anonymous information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • General geographic location
  • Device or browser type

This information does not identify individual visitors.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve the browsing experience and collect basic analytics data.

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help the website function properly.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

How Your Information Is Stored

Any personal information you provide, such as through email or contact forms, is stored securely and only kept for as long as necessary to respond to enquiries or maintain records.

Your Data Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the email address listed above.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
